ABOUT US

A market leader in Anti Bird Nets, Agro Shade Nets, Safety Nets, Rodent Nets etc.

Established at Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India, "Shaksham Birdnet Company" is a leading manufacturer, supplier and exporter of nets and stainless steel spikes used for varied applications.Some of the end usage of these nets include for construction sites, in agricultural fields, for use as insect screens, for fishing purposes and others. The wide range company is presently offering include Construction Site Nets, Safety Net, Hammock Nets, Fishing Nets, Bird Protection Net, Mosquitoes Net, Agro Shade Net, Stainless Steel Spikes, Rat Rodent Net, Anti Insect Screens and Packaging Nets Anti Birdnet.

Backed by rich industry experience, the team at “Shaksham Birdnet Company” is able to provide these in best available design and construction standards as per the specific demands of the customers. Well defined production measures are also undertaken by the company.
